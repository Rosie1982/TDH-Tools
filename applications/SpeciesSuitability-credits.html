Credits

This tool was developed by a team at JCU's eResearch Centre and uses data from the Atlas of Living Australia. The source code is available on github.

The principal researcher and project advisor was Dr Jeremy VanDerWal.

The project that produced this tool and several others was supported by the Australian National Data Service (ANDS) through the National Collaborative Research Infrastructure Strategy Program and the Education Investment Fund (EIF) Super Science Initiative, as well as through the Queensland Cyber Infrastructure Foundation (QCIF).
